About The Co-Ed Softball Life Group

Looking for a fun and meaningful way to spend your Saturday? Our life group is more than just a game—it’s a community. We gather every Saturday to enjoy a friendly game of softball, lift each other up in prayer, and grow together in faith.

Whether you’re an experienced player or just looking for fellowship, you are welcome here! Our time together is about more than just sports—it’s about building friendships, encouraging one another, and strengthening our walk with Christ.

Join us for a great time of connection, encouragement, and fun. Bring your glove, your heart for God, and let’s grow together!
